Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Auto Poster ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our application and services.

1. Information We Collect

Our application is designed to automate content publishing to social media platforms. We collect and process only the minimum information necessary to provide this service:

  • Facebook Page Access Tokens: Used solely to publish content to your authorized Facebook Pages.
  • YouTube Channel URLs: Used to discover publicly available video content for reposting.

We do not collect personal information from end users, visitors, or followers of the Facebook Pages managed through our service.

2. How We Use Information

The information we process is used exclusively for:

  • Fetching publicly available video content from YouTube channels.
  • Publishing video content to authorized Facebook Pages via the Facebook Graph API.
  • Maintaining a history of published content to prevent duplicate posts.

3. Data Storage

Access tokens and configuration data are stored securely as encrypted environment variables within our deployment infrastructure (GitHub Actions Secrets). We do not store tokens in plain text or share them with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our application interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Facebook Graph API: For publishing content to Facebook Pages.
  • RapidAPI: For accessing YouTube video download services.
  • GitHub Actions: For automated workflow execution.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Data is only shared with the platforms listed above as necessary to provide our service.

6. Data Retention

We retain published video history records (video IDs only) to prevent duplicate content. No personal data is retained. Access tokens are stored only as long as they are needed for the service to function.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect information, including encrypted secret storage, secure HTTPS connections, and access controls.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke access to your Facebook Page at any time through Facebook Settings.
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your account.
  • Opt out of our service at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
